INTERESTING MOVIES

[THAT DOROTHY J. KOECH WATCHED]

  • My Wife & Kids

    "My Wife And Kids", is a movie series comedy of a husband and his wife that stands as the anchor of love and resilience, while his kids are the vibrant colors that paint each chapter with laughter and adventure. Together, they are the cherished stars that illuminate their world with boundless joy and warmth.

  • Homeless to Harvard

    "Homeless to Harvard", is a moving story about a girl who overcomes incredible challenges to achieve her dream of going to Harvard University, despite growing up without a home.

  • Sister Act

    In "Sister Act", follow the hilarious escapades of Deloris Van Cartier, a sassy lounge singer forced into hiding as a nun after witnessing a crime. With uproarious humor and toe-tapping musical numbers, it's a feel-good comedy that will have you singing along and laughing out loud.

  • The Gods Must Be Crazy

    "The Gods Must Be Crazy", is a whimsical adventure that follows a San bushman's journey after discovering a Coca-Cola bottle believed to be a divine gift. Chaos ensues among his tribe, leading him on a quest to return the disruptive object to the gods at the edge of the world.

Developed by: Dorothy J. Koech
